## ğŸ§® 2. **Top-k Sampling**

### ğŸ“Œ What is it?

Instead of sampling from all tokens, you **restrict to the top `k` highest-probability tokens**, and **sample only from them**.

### ğŸ”§ How it works:


### ğŸ“ˆ Benefit:

* Keeps sampling fast and focused
* Still allows variation, unlike greedy
